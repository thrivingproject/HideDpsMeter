# HideDpsMeter

Hides Blizzard's built-in Damage Meter while you are in combat, then shows it again the moment combat ends.

## Why?

We all want big numbers. This addon lets you lock in feel the intensity and suspense of pumping the meters throughout the entire fight and then shows you the results when it's over. Watching live number ticking up or down triggers little hits of pride or frustration — or make you mess up mechanics — so this addon lets you delay the gratification until after you get the kill.

## What it does

- On entering combat, any visible Blizzard Damage Meter window is hidden.
- On leaving combat, the windows that were hidden are shown again.
- Only meters that were already open get re-shown — it will not pop the meter open for you.

## Toggle

Open the game menu → **Options** → **AddOns** → **HideDpsMeter**, and use the checkbox to turn the behavior on or off. Your choice persists across sessions.
